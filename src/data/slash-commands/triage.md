---
description: Triage and prioritize pending tasks
---
Review all my non-completed tasks and help me triage them. For each task, consider:

1. **Priority assessment** — is the current priority (immediate/important/backlog/none) correct?
2. **Staleness** — are there tasks that haven't been touched in a while and should be closed or deprioritized?
3. **Quick wins** — identify tasks that could be completed quickly
4. **Dependencies** — are any tasks blocked by other tasks?

Group the output by recommended action:
- **Do now** — high-impact tasks that should be tackled immediately
- **Schedule** — important but not urgent, suggest when to work on them
- **Delegate/Automate** — tasks that could be handled by a session
- **Drop/Archive** — tasks that are stale or no longer relevant

Be opinionated and direct. If something should be dropped, say so.
